About Us

Leading manufacturer and supplier of kitchen appliances

Established in the year 1995, we, Gruha Lakshmi Kitchen Accessories, Vijayawada, have achieved a reputed name in the industry as manufacturer of an extensive range of Kitchen Baskets and also a Distributor & Dealer for Chimneys, Hobs, Sinks. Manufactured using quality range, our gamut is widely acclaimed for sturdiness, durability, longevity, and smooth finishing.

QUALITY ASSURANCE :

Our undivided commitment towards providing high quality range of Kitchen Baskets and Accessories has made us earn an acknowledged name in this industry. From the procurement of raw material till the final delivery, we keep a stringent control on the durability and efficiency. Our quality controllers undertakes several measures to make sure that our range is at par with the industry standards. They check kitchenware based on following parameters :

  • Durability
  • Dimensional accuracy
  • Corrosion resistance
  • Design
  • Portability

WHY US?

Our extensive industry experience supported us to emerge as leading companies in the kitchenware sector. At present, we offer our range to various hotels, restaurants, homes and office canteens. Following factors supported us in craving a niche for ourselves in the industry :

  • Use of superior quality raw material
  • Customization solutions
  • Timely delivery
  • Ethical business practices
  • Quality surveillance at various levels of production
